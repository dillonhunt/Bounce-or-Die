Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Bounce or Die?

Tap the left side of the screen to bounce left, or the right side to bounce right. Land on platforms to score points and survive. The game gets faster and platforms get smaller as your score increases!

How do I unlock new skins?

There are several ways to unlock skins:

  • Score-based: Reach certain high scores to unlock Ember (25) and Toxic (50)
  • Coins: Collect coins during gameplay and spend them in the shop
  • Premium: Purchase exclusive skins like Diamond, Phantom, and more

I made a purchase but didn't receive my items!

First, try the "Restore Purchases" button in the shop. This will restore any previously purchased items. If that doesn't work, please ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. If issues persist, contact us with your purchase receipt.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Open the Shop from the home screen and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID that was used for the original purchases.

I lost my game progress! Can you help?

Unfortunately, game progress (scores, coins earned through gameplay, score-unlocked skins) is stored locally on your device and cannot be recovered if deleted. However, any purchased items can always be restored using "Restore Purchases."

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID. We cannot process refunds directly.

The game is lagging or crashing!

Try these steps:

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure your device has the latest iOS version
  • Delete and reinstall the app (note: this will erase local progress)

If issues persist, please contact us with your device model and iOS version.

What are the perfect landing bonuses?

Land in the center of a platform (within 15% of the platform's center) to earn bonus points! You'll see "+CENTER" appear and hear a special sound. This is a great way to rack up higher scores.

Pro Tips

  • Green platforms give you a super boost - use them to climb quickly!
  • Red crumbling platforms break after one landing - plan your next move!
  • Purple moving platforms are worth 3 points - risk vs reward!
  • Tiny platforms are also worth 3 points - aim carefully!
  • Collect coins to unlock new skins in the shop

System Requirements

  • iOS 17.0 or later
  • iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch
  • Approximately 50 MB of storage space

Report a Bug

Found a bug or glitch? We'd love to hear about it so we can fix it! When reporting a bug, please include:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro)
  • Your iOS version (e.g., iOS 17.2)
  • A description of what happened
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (if possible)
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if applicable)
