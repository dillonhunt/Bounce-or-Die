Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Ember Labs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Bounce or Die mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Information Automatically Collected

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Game scores, gameplay statistics, features used, and interaction patterns
  • Purchase Information: Records of in-app purchases processed through Apple's App Store

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect contacts or phone data
  • We do not require account creation
  • We do not collect data from children under 13

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Process in-app purchases and restore previous purchases
  • Save your game progress and preferences locally on your device
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Data Storage

Your game data (scores, unlocked skins, coin balance, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard data persistence mechanisms. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

If you delete the App or clear its data, your local game progress will be permanently lost.

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For processing in-app purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies to payment processing. Visit apple.com/legal/privacy for more information.
  • Apple Game Center (if enabled): For leaderboards and achievements. Apple's privacy policy governs this service.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is rated for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require any personal information to play. In-app purchases require parental permission through Apple's built-in parental controls.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your local game data by uninstalling the App
  • Disable analytics through your device settings
  • Request information about data we may have collected
  • Opt out of any future data collection

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not collect personal information, these rights are largely not applicable, but you may still contact us with any questions.

10. International Users

If you are accessing our App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.
